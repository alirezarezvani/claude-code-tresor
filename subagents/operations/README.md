## Overview

The Operations team agents provide comprehensive expertise in business operations, analytics, infrastructure management, and customer support. These agents help optimize operational efficiency, analyze business metrics, and ensure reliable service delivery.

### Team Specializations

- **Analytics** - Business analytics, revenue analysis, metrics reporting
- **Infrastructure** - System health monitoring, performance optimization, operational excellence
- **Support** - Customer support, ticket handling, support documentation
