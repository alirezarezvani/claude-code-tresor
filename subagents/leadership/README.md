## Overview

The Leadership team agents provide comprehensive expertise in financial planning, business strategy, risk management, and regulatory compliance. These agents help make informed business decisions, optimize financial performance, and ensure regulatory adherence.

### Team Specializations

- **Finance** - Financial analysis, budget planning, cost optimization, investment evaluation
- **Business Strategy** - Strategic planning, competitive positioning, partnership development
- **Risk Management** - Risk modeling, scenario planning, business continuity
- **Compliance** - Regulatory compliance, legal documentation, policy development
