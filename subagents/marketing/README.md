## Overview

The Marketing team agents provide comprehensive expertise in content marketing, social media, growth hacking, and SEO optimization. These agents help drive user acquisition, engagement, and brand awareness across multiple channels.

### Team Specializations

- **Content Marketing** - Blog posts, newsletters, long-form content, copywriting
- **Social Media** - Platform-specific strategies for Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit
- **Growth Hacking** - Rapid user acquisition, viral loops, data-driven experiments
- **SEO & ASO** - Search engine optimization and app store optimization
